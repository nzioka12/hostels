How to Book (simple steps)

  1. Search for a hostel or click a listing from the table above.
  2. Check the photos and the price displayed in the preview card.
  3. Contact the hostel using the phone/email or click "View on map" to confirm availability.
  4. Pay a deposit (if required) and confirm your dates.
  5. Arrive at the hostel and show booking confirmation.

Tip: keep a screenshot or PDF of your booking confirmation.
